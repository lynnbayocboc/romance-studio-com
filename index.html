The Rich woman's Daughter.

This a travel romance novel about the relationship between a mother and daughter. The mother is a wealthy business woman in the Philippines who wants only the best for her daughter. Her daughter seeks independence, travels to Thailand to escape an arranged marriage, and falls in love with a handsome British man. Her future looks bright until she gets caught up in drug smuggling, sent to prison, escapes during a riot and is kidnapped by a criminal gang. She returns to the Philippines as a criminal but seeks to reunite with her mother and find her long-lost true love. The novel is both a thriller and a romance, for a fun read, but it touches on topics of importance to women in the developing world and beyond, including conflicts between romantic love and family expectations, the complicated relationships of mothers and daughters, and womenâ€™s struggle for empowerment.
